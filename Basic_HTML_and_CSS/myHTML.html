Unicorns

Unicorns are magical horses that are distinguished by a single horn on their forehead. Some cultures believe them to have supernatural powers. In Harry Potter, unicorn blood will save your life if you are about to die.


Pegasi

Pegasi are winged horses named after the Greek creature named Pegasus. Some of their powers include walking on clouds and controlling weather.


Hippogriff

Hippogriffs have the head of an eagle and body of a horse. They are extremely fast and ready to protect. It is thought to be the symbol of love.
